The Classic PB&J

Serving size: 1 sandwich

Ingredients

Peanut Butter: 2 tablespoons

Jelly/Jam: 1.5 tablespoons (we used 2 tablespoons) (now using 1.5 for a better ratio)

Bread: 2 slices

Butter (optional): 1 teaspoon

Banana Slices (optional): 1/2 a banana

Instructions

Step 1: Lay the 2 slices of bread side-by-side on a clean surface.

Step 2: Spread **2 tablespoons** of peanut butter on one slice. *Ensure you go all the way to the edges* to prevent dry bites.

Step 3: Spread **1.5 tablespoons** of jelly on the other slice. *Don't overdo it*, or the sandwich will be soggy.

Step 4: Carefully place the peanut butter slice on top of the jelly slice, peanut butter down, so the spreads meet.

Step 5: Slice the sandwich diagonally. This is purely for presentation.

Chef's Notes

lightly toast the bread *before* spreading. **Always use fresh bread.** Stale bread ruins the experience. Adding a sprinkle of sea salt to the peanut butter side can enhance the flavor.
